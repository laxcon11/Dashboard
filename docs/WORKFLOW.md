# Daily Trading Workflow 📉

This document outlines a structured daily routine using the NSE Swing Trading Dashboard. Follow this checklist to maintain discipline and consistency.

## 1. 🌅 Pre-Market Routine (8:45 AM - 9:15 AM)
**Objective**: Establish situational awareness and shortlist focus sectors.

### Step 1: Global Sentiment Check
*   **Open**: `1_Global_Markets` page.
*   **Check**:
    *   **S&P 500 / NASDAQ Futures**: Green or Red? (Directional Bias)
    *   **DXY (Dollar Index)**: Rising > 103? (Risk Off) vs Falling? (Risk On)
    *   **US 10Y Yields**: Spiking? (Bad for Tech/Growth)
*   **Decision**: Is today likely to be a **BUY** day, **SELL** day, or **CHOPPY** day?

### Step 2: Sector Focus
*   **Open**: `0_NSE_Dashboard` -> Select **Morning Review**.
*   **Check**: "Sectoral Performance".
*   **Action**: Identify the top 2 strongest sectors and bottom 2 weakest sectors from yesterday. Focus your trades here.

---

### 1. Morning Review (09:15 AM - 09:30 AM)
*   **Goal**: Identify overnight momentum or potential breakouts.
*   **Action**:
    1.  Switch to **"Morning Review"** mode.
    2.  Check the **Gap Analysis** table for stocks gapping up > 0.5% with high volume.
    3.  Check the **Volatility Contraction (NR7)** table for stocks coiling for a big move.
    4.  *Optional*: Note down any stocks breaking their 5-day high.

### 2. End of Day Review (03:30 PM - 04:00 PM)
*   **Goal**: Filter stocks for the next day's watchlist.
*   **Action**:
    1.  Switch to **"End of Day"** mode.
    2.  Review "Above VWAP" list for strength.
    3.  Check **Advance/Decline Ratio** to gauge sector sentiment.
    4.  Use **"Swing Rankings"** mode to find setups:
        *   **Momentum**: Look for scores > 7 in the *Breakout Candidates* table (Green).
        *   **Pullback**: Look for scores > 7 in the *Value Candidates* table (Blue).

### 3. Trade Planning (Anytime)
*   **Goal**: Execute a trade with proper risk management.
*   **Action**:
    1.  Switch to **"Full Analysis"** mode and select your target stock.
    2.  Check the **Smart Risk Planner**:
        *   Enter your **Max Risk (Loss)** amount (e.g., ₹2000).
        *   Select Stop Loss Strategy (e.g., "2x ATR").
        *   Note the **Quantity** and **Capital Required**.
    3.  Verify Price is above VWAP on the chart.

### Step 5: Technical Confirmation
*   **Open**: `0_NSE_Dashboard` -> Select **Full Analysis**.
*   **Select**: The top candidates from Step 4.
*   **Verify**:
    *   **RSI**: Between 50-70? (Momentum is strong but not overextended).
    *   **Structure**: Is price above 20 EMA?
    *   **Risk**: Check "ATR Stop Loss". Is the risk acceptable?

---

## 4. 🌆 Post-Market Routine (3:45 PM - 4:15 PM)
**Objective**: Review and Journal.

### Step 6: Market Breadth Review
*   **Open**: `0_NSE_Dashboard` -> Select **End of Day**.
*   **Check**:
    *   **Advance/Decline Ratio**: Was the broad market participating?
    *   **VWAP Stats**: Are the majority of stocks closing above VWAP? (Bullish confirmation).

### Step 7: Journaling (Live)
*   **Action**: Log your trades in the `5_Trading_Journal` page.

*   **Record**:
    *   Entry Price
    *   Exit Price (if closed)
    *   Mistakes made / Lessons learned.

---

## 📌 Rules of Engagement
1.  **No Data = No Trade**: If the dashboard isn't loading or data is old, do not guess.
2.  **Trend is King**: Do not fight the "Sector Performance" direction.
3.  **VWAP Filter**: For intraday/swing, being Above VWAP is a non-negotiable for Longs.
